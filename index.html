About Me

Hey there! I'm Joey.

For 10 years, I was deep in the hustle of the hospitality industry—running restaurants, managing teams, and seeing way too many businesses fail simply because they weren’t using their data right. That’s when I started taking on jobs as a food and beverage business consultant, digging into the numbers to find ways to cut waste, boost sales, and keep the doors open.

Turns out, I loved the analytics side so much that I went back to school. Now, I’m leveling up with a degree in Global Business Management and a specialist diploma in Business Analytics & Applied Data Science—because in today’s world, gut instinct isn’t enough. You need data to back it up.

My mission? To be the consultant who doesn’t just hand you a spreadsheet, but actually helps you use it to grow your business. Whether it’s spotting trends, optimizing operations, or just figuring out why your best-selling product isn’t as profitable as it should be, I’m all about turning numbers into real-world wins.
